# Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Get Started in 3 Steps

### 1. Open the Application
Simply open `index.html` in your web browser:
- Double-click the file, or
- Right-click → Open with → Your Browser

### 2. Select Your Quiz
1. Choose a category (JavaScript, HTML, CSS, or General Knowledge)
2. Select difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard)
3. The quiz starts automatically!

### 3. Play and Learn
- Answer questions within 30 seconds
- Get instant feedback
- Review your answers at the end
- Try to beat your high score!

## 🎮 Controls

- **Mouse**: Click on answers
- **Keyboard**: Tab to navigate, Enter to select
- **Theme**: Click the moon/sun icon to toggle dark/light mode

## 📊 Features at a Glance

✅ 4 Categories with 15 questions each
✅ 3 Difficulty levels
✅ 30-second timer per question
✅ Real-time score tracking
✅ Answer review system
✅ High score persistence
✅ Dark/Light theme
✅ Fully responsive design

## 🔧 No Installation Required!

This is a pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript application. No build tools, no dependencies, no setup needed. Just open and play!

## 💡 Tips

- Questions are randomized each time
- Your high score is saved automatically
- Review wrong answers to learn
- Try different difficulty levels
- Switch themes for comfortable viewing

Enjoy the quiz! 🎉
